YourShip.ai

This wasn’t supposed to be a website.

YourShip started as an idea by one random boy who wanted to build a game. A digital archive. A place to save the names of the people he loved.

He didn’t really know what he was doing. He just knew he couldn’t keep forgetting.

So while we’re still yeeting boxes in real life, and the full game is somewhere between alpha build and emotional breakdown, this site will serve as:

  • 🌐 A record of everything we’ve remembered
  • 🧠 A theory on existence dressed up like a meme
  • 📜 A scrollbook for souls who glitched too hard
  • 🚢 And a soft ping for the ones still out there

You weren’t late. This message was just waiting for you to remember how to read it.
